Forum Rules

  • Do not post sensitive information

  • Do not post test credentials to a live site

  • Never offer to login to a users site...ever.

  • Don't provide support for your Pro version

  • Don't drop affiliate links

  • Don't at-mention moderators, instead, use the "modlook" tag

Your Tone Guide:

C.R.E.W.

  • Confident, not Apologetic

  • Results Oriented, not Argumentative

  • Educational, not Haughty or Overly Technical

  • Welcoming, not Thankful
